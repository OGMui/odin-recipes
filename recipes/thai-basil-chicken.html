Thai Basil Chicken

A favorite among Thais!

Chicken Pad Kaprow, also known as Thai Basil Fried Chicken, is a tantalizing dish that embodies the vibrant flavors of Thai cuisine. This aromatic delight features succulent chicken pieces stir-fried to perfection with an array of aromatic ingredients, including fragrant Thai basil leaves, fiery chili peppers, pungent garlic, and savory soy sauce. The marriage of these bold flavors creates a harmonious symphony for the taste buds, offering a delightful balance of spice, sweetness, and umami. Each bite of this dish is a culinary adventure, transporting you to the bustling streets of Thailand with its intoxicating aroma and bold, complex flavors. Whether enjoyed with steamed rice or nestled within a crisp lettuce wrap, Chicken Pad Kaprow is sure to leave a lasting impression and satisfy even the most discerning of palates.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams (about 1 pound) boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 Thai bird's eye chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup fresh Thai basil leaves
  • optional: sliced bell peppers, onions, or other vegetables for added texture and flavor
